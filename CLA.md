# Contributor License Agreement (stub)

Telaradio is licensed under AGPL-3.0. To preserve the option of
relicensing or dual-licensing the project in the future, contributors will
be asked to sign a Contributor License Agreement before their changes are
merged.

**Status**: the formal CLA process is not yet active. Contribution opens in
Phase 2 (see [`ROADMAP.md`](ROADMAP.md)). Until then, please open issues
rather than PRs.

When the CLA process is active, expect a one-time sign-off via a CLA bot on
your first PR. The agreement will:

- License your contribution under AGPL-3.0 (the project's current license).
- Grant the project maintainers permission to relicense your contribution
  under a different open-source license, or to dual-license it, if the
  project decides that is necessary in the future.
- Confirm you have the right to make the contribution.

The CLA does not transfer copyright. You retain copyright in your
contribution.

If this approach is a blocker for you, please open an issue — happy to
discuss alternatives.
